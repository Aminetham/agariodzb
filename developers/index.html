OGARio Developers - The OGARio ongoing project



Welcome to the Discord community for developing custom OGARio scripts

Please note:

We tag our developers as:
Guests - (skillful developers)
Developers (Ogario developers) - Very good and selected developers, who can prove their work on agar.io and know about agar.io protocols

Guest:

1. A developer can join our community and ask for question. After been answered, he is obliged to leave our discord server.
2. A well known developer who is reserved in order to answer question if needed.
3. Active developers sharing their scripts.

Ogario developer:

These are the developers who will improve the real ogario.js and ogario.master.js code.

Rules for the Ogario developers:

1. Must NOT and under no circumstances reveal our decoded ogario.js and ogario.master.js files to others.
2. Must NOT keep their updated ogario.js and ogario.master.js files for themselves, but share it in this room.
3. Must be active on our Discord server and improve the files.
4. Must not use scripts that ruin agario.

Ogario developers also:

Have their own scripts, which is their own work that combine with ogario.js or not.
Their scripts must not use Canvas rendering or similar stuff from our files, but rather use it in our files.

The OGARio Discord community is an ongoing project

On this project hard work is done, which belongs to this community only.
* If a guest does not comply with rules, he will be removed.
** If Ogario developer will not comply with rules, he will be permanently banned with no access to the ongoing project.
