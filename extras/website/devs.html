Basic Strategy


FFA has a SOLO-like gameplay, players must not W, split or merge with other players much.
If someone is new to the clan we must understand that his/her main role is to split the enemy by shooting viruses.
Do not merge much after 2000 points between you, do not merge often and don't give your half part to the big cell without reason.
Players between 2000-8000 should split eating the enemy, split to virus and give mass to small teammates and W to very small.
They also have to merge with themselves and not with the biggest cell. Players with mass bigger than 8000 must not W to smaller should not feed the very small.
If they are spit by a virus shooter then they give their smaller parts to a teammate.

The very massive players, "try" at first to find an enemy to split before you split 16.

Try NOT to get ANTI and loose Clan mass. Anti-Team is when your cell begins to shrink.

These are common mistakes players make which get them into Anti:

1. Splitting too quickly. FFA is designed for slow gameplay. So when you split, merge into your teammate and split again too frequently, you will get yourself into Anti.
In short - don't press SPACE too much and please slow down!
2. The W (mass feed) key. Agar.io FFA will detect if someone is pressing W too much. As a rule of thumb, try not to 'W' away more than 200 mass if it can be helped.
If you need to feed your teammate, purposefully split yourself onto a virus, and allow your clan teammate to eat your small cells.
3. Splitting too much on viruses. The splitting on viruses technique to give your teammate mass cannot be used too quickly.
Leave a gap of at least 1 minute before splitting yourself on a virus again. If you split on 2 viruses consecutively, your cell will decay and shrink very fast.
4. Splitting for more mass when in Anti. If you are already in Anti, do not split or press W. Just wait for your anti to go away. Usually this will take 1 minute.
If you split for a piece because you can't resist the temptation (we've all been there) it's likely you will just lose the mass you just split for because your anti will increase.

If you get into ANTI, give your mass to a teammate and leave yourself around 500 mass. If your teammate does not need you then you can disconnect and reconnect to server.

There have reported many cases of Clan members who never help other member. Others prefer playing with random friends or allies than Clan players,
or teaming with players who attack other Clan members. CLAN GOES FIRST, RESPECT CLAN MEMBERS, as also they respect that you should not get ANTI.
HELP all Clan members, give them mass after a while, or even W them once to show them that you want to help them but you cannot because you will get ANTI.

PLAYERS WHO DO NOT RESPECT THESE RULES DO NOT BELONG TO THIS CLAN.

Pro Strategy FFA

Important notices:

You must be aware of the co-player you play
Play like party, but you need a full understanding on the anti that will occur/
A cell must have a size of 30% more of target cell in order to eat it. For example target cell has a mass of 100.
Your cell must be at least 130, otherwise your cell just wanders around the target cell and nothing happens despite you are bigger.
Mass, in this game is the area of cell indeed. So the diameter of the hunter cell must be 10% longer than the target cell (sqrt(1,30) ~ 1.13).

Let’s calculate “eating by splitting” case. Asume that target cell has a mass of 100. Then your eating-cell must be at least 130.
So your original size must be at least 260(130 x 2). Consider this carefully because you should not split your cell unnecessarily or you will be a target for others.

Clan Elite Play (Members Only)

DO NOT TRUST random players, play with the clan players who use your tag and the ingame communication.

Keeping a dominated server is much easier than joining a server dominated by another clan.

Although, 8 players can easily dominate a server that is dominated by another clan. When members play against another good clan they must discuss their strategy at first.

Members can watch the enemy's clans friends, visit the enemy's clan website and see their friends and their members.
All members must join with the same tag to use ingame communication. Then 2 members can use enemy clan's friends names and skin, W them and show they are friendly.
1 member can try to be a random player who shows them he wants to help them and 1 other member can copy the enemy clan's name.

The other 4 members who use our symbol can gradualy take the mass. Spies need to be undercover, they mustn't show who they are.
After server is dominated to clan, these members can use their normal names with clan's symbol.

A player can either invite his friend to skype room directly (as an admin, because some players are banned), or use our strategy technique on recruiting.
