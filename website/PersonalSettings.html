Personal Settings

You can choose your personal settings for the game.

There are plenty ways you can do this:

  • One Click Settings
  • Import Settings
  • Or Make your own settings

 

One Click Settings

Import Settings with just one Click on the helloscreen.

 

Import Settings

Some settings you may import manually by clicking on the tab Settings->Export Import Settings->"Copy paste the CSS code" to the Import Settings box->Click Import Settings 

CSS Settings (click for code):

  • VanillaBetter
  • VanillaLike
  • Galaxy
  • Ogariodefault
  • Agario

 

Make your own Settings

Make your own settings and Save them. Share them if you like
